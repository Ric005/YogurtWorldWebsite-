Store Directions

YogurtWorld is located at the northwest corner of the Student Services Center on the UC San Diego campus.

The nearest cross street to the Student Services Center is Myers Drive and Rupertus Lane.

Student Services Center, 9500 Gilman Dr, La Jolla, CA 92093

Parking Instructions

The nearest paid parking is available at the Gilman Parking Structure. it is 5 minute walking distance away from our YogurtWorld location. The first hour at Gilman Parking Structure is free for visitors.

There are also free, limited parking spots in the 30 minute loading zone between Raisel Lane and Lyman Lane.

3100 Gilman Dr, La Jolla, CA 92093
